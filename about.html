About us

With a contemporary and local ambience, we strive to be community minded while showcasing our undiscovered bayside dining experience and events centre. Using only fresh ingredients, our chef's make everything possible in-house, from cured meats to freshly rolled pasta and buns, right down to the ketchup and mayonnaise, to create our gourmet comfort foods. This is your place to enjoy with friends and family, while you sit back, relax, and watch the sailboats pass by.
